"Why hello there!" You are greeted by a cat.
It sits on the table.
"It's about time you've woken up, i've been waiting all morning for you."
The cat is black.
He gets off of the table and stands on his hind legs, but that is not the most astonishing thing: he is almost 2 meters tall.
The cat pours warm rooibos tea into a dainty tea cup, the claw of his pinky pings out appropriately.
His yellow eyes are hypnotic. You find yourself no longer caring about his size.
His grin is devilishly adorable. "Care for a cup?"
