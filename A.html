'those who speak in tongues shall be blessed'

You wake up from your bed and approach the window.
You now realise why you thought it was still dark outside.
Something is blocking the window.
Your window is a relatively small one,
so you cannot make out exactly what or who
is obstructing your view.

Suddenly words begin to form upon the darkened glass. You rush to your bedside for a quill and pad and return to the opaque glass to take down the following set of glyphs

  • nruter
  • esaelp
  • uoy
  • simm
  • i

You have not encountered this tongue before. However something inside you burns as you stare at the gibberish on the page. You look up to the window and see nothing but the bright of day.
